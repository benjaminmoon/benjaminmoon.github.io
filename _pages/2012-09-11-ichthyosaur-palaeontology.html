Ichthyosaur taxonomy

Taxonomy is the classification of living things. This page has a list of ichthyosaur genera and species: each genus may contain one or more species.

Ichthyosaur systematics

Systematics is related to taxonomy, both is concerned with classification. Here systematics will be used to cover the higher level taxonomy; how ichthyosaurs are related to each other.

Ichthyosaur biology

The biology of ichthyosaurs has been widely discussed over many years. This page will showcase some of the theories.
